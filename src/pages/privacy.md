## 1. What Payxem Does

Payxem is an invoicing and financial tracking platform. We enable users to create invoices, send payment links, track payments, and manage earnings.

Payments are processed through third-party providers such as PayPal and Stripe. Payxem does not operate as a bank or licensed financial institution.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services.

### a. Information You Provide

- Name and profile details
- Email address
- Phone number (optional)
- Business or service information
- Bank or payout details (when provided)
- Identity verification information (for KYC, when required)

### b. Transaction and Usage Data

- Invoice details (amounts, descriptions, client data)
- Payment records and statuses
- Wallet and ledger activity
- Withdrawal and payout requests

### c. Device and Technical Data

- IP address
- Browser type and device information
- Usage logs and activity within the platform

### d. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

- Keep you logged in
- Improve performance
- Understand usage patterns

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

- Provide and operate Payxem services
- Create and manage invoices and payment records
- Process withdrawals and payouts
- Verify identity (KYC where required)
- Improve platform performance and security
- Communicate important updates and support responses
- Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized activity

## 4. Payment Processing

All payments are handled by third-party providers such as PayPal and Stripe.

- Payment data may be processed directly by these providers
- Payxem may receive payment status and transaction details
- Funds may be received into accounts designated by Payxem or its affiliated entities for settlement and distribution

Payxem does not provide banking or custodial financial services.

## 5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share information with:

### a. Payment Providers

To process transactions and verify payments.

### b. Service Providers

Hosting, analytics, email delivery, and infrastructure services.

### c. Identity Verification Partners

For KYC and compliance checks (when required).

### d. Legal Requirements

If required by law, regulation, or to protect rights and safety.

## 6. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data, including:

- Encrypted connections (HTTPS)
- Secure authentication and session management
- Access controls and monitoring
- Regular system updates

However, no system is completely secure. You are responsible for keeping your login credentials safe.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as necessary to:

- Provide services
- Maintain financial and transaction records
- Comply with legal obligations

You may request deletion of your account, subject to legal and operational requirements.

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- Access your personal data

- Request deletion of your data
- Object to certain data uses

To make a request, contact us at: [support@payxem.com](mailto:support@payxem.com)

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. You should review their policies separately.

## 10. International Use

Payxem is designed for global use, including regions where traditional payment systems may be limited.

By using Payxem, you acknowledge that your data may be processed in multiple countries where our services or partners operate.
