## Prohibited Activities

You may not use Payxem for any of the following:

**Illegal Goods or Services**
- Sale or distribution of illegal drugs, weapons, or controlled substances
- Counterfeit products, pirated software, or stolen goods
- Unlicensed financial services or investment schemes
- Any activity that violates local, national, or international law

**Fraudulent or Deceptive Practices**
- Creating fake invoices or charging for services not delivered
- Identity theft or impersonation
- Money laundering or structuring transactions to avoid reporting requirements
- Misrepresenting yourself, your business, or the nature of transactions
- Processing payments on behalf of another business without authorization

**Restricted Industries**
- Gambling, betting, or casino operations
- Adult content or services
- Multi-level marketing or pyramid schemes
- Cryptocurrency exchanges or investment platforms (unless pre-approved)
- High-risk financial services

**Abusive Behavior**
- Harassment, threats, or abuse directed at other users or our support team
- Spam, unsolicited commercial messages, or phishing attempts
- Attempting to access another user's account without permission
- Interfering with the platform's security or functionality

**Sanctions and Embargoes**
- Transactions involving countries, entities, or individuals subject to economic sanctions
- Violations of export controls or trade embargoes

---

## User Responsibilities

**You Must:**
- Provide accurate and complete information when creating your account
- Use your real identity and business details
- Deliver goods or services as described in your invoices
- Respond to customer disputes in good faith
- Maintain the security of your account credentials
- Comply with all applicable laws in your jurisdiction

**You Must Not:**
- Share your account access with unauthorized parties
- Create multiple accounts to circumvent platform limits
- Attempt to manipulate or abuse our fee structure
- Reverse engineer or attempt to compromise our platform

---

## Compliance Requirements

**Know Your Customer**
You are responsible for verifying the identity of your clients and ensuring transactions are legitimate.

**Tax Obligations**
You must comply with all tax reporting and payment obligations in your jurisdiction. Payxem does not provide tax advice.

**Data Protection**
You must handle customer data in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

**Payment Processor Terms**
You must also comply with the terms of service of the payment processors you use through Payxem (such as card processors and payment gateways).

## Monitoring and Enforcement

**We Reserve the Right To:**
- Monitor transactions for suspicious activity
- Request additional information or documentation to verify compliance
- Suspend or terminate accounts that violate this policy
- Report illegal activity to law enforcement or regulatory authorities
- Withhold funds pending investigation of policy violations
- Refuse service to any user at our discretion

**We Are Not Required To:**
- Monitor all user activity or transactions
- Investigate every dispute or complaint
- Provide advance notice before suspending an account for serious violations

---

## Account Suspension or Termination

If you violate this Acceptable Use Policy, we may:

**Immediate Actions:**
- Suspend your account and freeze pending withdrawals
- Block access to the platform
- Withhold funds for investigation

**Permanent Actions:**
- Terminate your account
- Forfeit any pending balances for policy violations involving fraud or illegal activity
- Report violations to payment processors, which may affect your ability to use their services elsewhere

**You Will Be Notified:**
We will notify you of account suspension or termination via the email address on file, except where doing so would compromise an ongoing investigation or legal proceeding.

---

## Appeals and Disputes

If you believe your account was suspended or terminated in error:

1. Contact us at support@payxem.com within 30 days
2. Provide evidence supporting your appeal
3. We will review and respond within 10 business days

Our decision after review is final.

## Consequences of Violations

Violations of this policy may result in:
- Temporary or permanent account suspension
- Forfeiture of funds obtained through prohibited activities
- Legal action, including civil claims or criminal prosecution
- Reporting to regulatory authorities or law enforcement
- Sharing of violation details with payment processors and financial institutions
