## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, improve your experience, and provide analytics about how the site is used.

Payxem primarily uses **local storage** and **session storage** (similar browser technologies) rather than traditional cookies. These work the same way but are managed by your browser and are not sent to servers with every request.

---

## Storage We Use

### **Essential Storage (Required)**

These are necessary for the platform to function. You cannot opt out of these.

| Storage Name | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| Authentication Token | Keeps you logged in and verifies your identity | Until logout |
| User Profile | Caches your name and role for display | Until logout |
| PIN Lock Hash | Stores your security PIN (hashed, not readable) | Until removed |
| Biometric Credential | Enables fingerprint/face login | Until disabled |
| Session Verification | Confirms PIN was entered this session | Session (closed with browser) |
| Service Worker Cache | Stores app files for fast loading and offline access | Until app update |

---

### **Functional Storage (Optional)**

These improve your experience but are not required for the platform to work.

| Storage Name | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| Install Banner Dismissed | Remembers if you dismissed the app install prompt | Permanent |
| Current Tab Selection | Remembers which section you were viewing | Session |

### **Analytics (Optional)**

We use analytics to understand how people use Payxem so we can improve the platform.

| Service | Used On | Purpose | Duration | Privacy Policy |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Umami Cloud | App + Website | Privacy-focused analytics: page views, device type, country | No cookies set (cookieless) | [Umami Privacy](https://umami.is/privacy) |
| Google Analytics | Website only (payxem.com) | Page views, user flow, referral source | 2 years | [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |

**What We Track:**
- Pages you visit
- Time spent on the platform
- Browser and device type
- Country and region (not exact location)
- How you found Payxem (referral source)

**What We DON'T Track:**
- Your personal information (name, email, address)
- Your financial data or transaction details
- Your exact location or IP address (beyond country/region)
- Anything across other websites you visit

**Note:** Umami is privacy-focused and does not use cookies. It collects anonymous usage data without tracking individual users.

## Managing Your Preferences

### **Essential Storage**
You cannot disable essential storage as it is required for the platform to function. If you clear this data, you will be logged out and need to sign in again.

### **Optional Storage**

**Browser Settings:**
Most browsers allow you to control cookies and local storage through their settings:
- [Chrome Cookie Settings](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Firefox Cookie Settings](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enhanced-tracking-protection-firefox-desktop)
- [Safari Cookie Settings](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-sfri11471/mac)
- [Edge Cookie Settings](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09)

**Opt Out of Analytics:**
- Google Analytics: [Google Analytics Opt-out](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
- Umami: No opt-out needed — Umami does not use cookies or track individual users

**Mobile Devices:**
- iOS: Settings > Safari > Block All Cookies
- Android: Settings > Site Settings > Cookies

## Do Not Track (DNT)

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" signal. We do not track users across other websites, so enabling DNT will not change how we collect data on Payxem.

**Service Worker Cache:** Cleared on app updates or when admin pushes an update
