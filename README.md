# Payxem

Professional Invoicing & Payments for Freelancers and Businesses

Website: https://app.payxem.com

---

## Overview

Payxem is a modern invoicing and payment management platform designed for freelancers, consultants, and small businesses.

It provides a simple way to create invoices, share payment links, and track earnings without the complexity of traditional tools or expensive subscriptions.

Payxem is built to work globally, with a strong focus on regions where access to international payment services is limited.

---

## Key Features

- Professional invoice creation with line items, tax, and due dates
- Secure payment links with multiple payment options
- Built-in ledger-style wallet for tracking earnings
- Client management with auto-fill and history
- Multi-currency support with live exchange rates
- Payment links and QR codes for fast collection
- Rewards system (XEM Points)
- Mobile-first PWA experience

---

## How It Works

1. Create your account  
2. Send an invoice or payment link  
3. Get paid and withdraw  

---

## Availability

Payxem is designed to work globally, especially in underserved markets such as Pakistan, Bangladesh, and similar regions.

If you have internet access, you can use Payxem.

---

## Pricing

- No monthly fees  
- No setup cost  
- Pay only per transaction  

Typical fee: 3.3% + $0.30 per payment received

---

## Important Disclaimer

Payxem is an invoicing and financial tracking platform, not a bank or payment processor.

Payments are processed through third-party providers such as PayPal and Stripe and may be received into accounts designated by Payxem or its affiliated entities for settlement and distribution.

Payxem does not provide banking or custodial financial services.
